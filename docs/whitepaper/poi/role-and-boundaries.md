# POI – Role & Boundaries (Public)

POI is a network value unit for coordination across ProjectEX and future network layers.

What POI is:
- A unit for fees/incentives/staking/governance across products and projects
- A coordination medium for cross‑project participation and access

What POI is not:
- Not company equity; not a claim to corporate profits
- Not an RWA revenue share; not a promise of returns

All economics parameters and distributions will be disclosed separately (when applicable) via dedicated technical/whitepaper releases.
