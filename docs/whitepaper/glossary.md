# Glossary (Public)

- Sign (连接钱包): establish on‑chain identity (akin to login)
- Approve (授权): permit a contract to spend tokens on your behalf
- Mint (铸造): create new on‑chain assets
- Burn (销毁): remove supply (deflationary mechanism)
- Stake (质押): lock value for rights/rewards
- Collateral (抵押): secure borrowing with assets
- Swap/Trade (兑换/交易): exchange assets
- LP (流动性池): provide inventory to earn fees
- On‑chain Record (链上记录): notarized public log
- Claim (空投领取): redeem entitlement into assets
